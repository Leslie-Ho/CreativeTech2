SPEEDY CUTTING SCISSORS

VIEW ON GITHUB

RULES OF THE GAME

The rules of the game are simple. Use the scissors to cut on the dotted line. Move your arms around to change the position of the scissors and open/close the scissors to start/stop the dotted lines. The goal is to get the highest points and the best accuracy as possible

MAKING THE SCISSOR CUTTING GAME

WHAT'S IN THE GAME?

In the game there are scissors, dotted lines, and your score and accuracy. As the game progresses, your score and time passed will dictate the level of difficulty. The more time has passed, the faster the dotted lines will move. The more points you have, the more erratic the dotted lines become.
