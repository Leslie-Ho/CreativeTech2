SPEEDY CUTTING SCISSORS

VIEW ON GITHUB

April 14, 2022

RULES OF THE GAME

The rules of the game are simple. Use the scissors to cut on the dotted line. Move your arms around to change the position of the scissors and open/close the scissors to start/stop the dotted lines. The goal is to get the highest points and the best accuracy as possible

MAKING THE SCISSOR CUTTING GAME

WHAT'S IN THE GAME?

In the game there are scissors, dotted lines, and your score and accuracy. As the game progresses, your score and time passed will dictate the level of difficulty. The more time has passed, the faster the dotted lines will move. The more points you have, the more erratic the dotted lines become.

INSPIRATIONS

When thinking about what to make, I was really in a sewing phase. I had been spending most of my free hours working on my other projects that involved sewing and was cutting so many fabrics that I thought it would be really funny if I made my own scissors. The idea didn't change much from there but I continued adding in degrees of complexity within the game itself and ended up creating the game I have now. Here are the innards of the sewing game. The pillow is stuffed with some extra Tshirts and I wrapped the bend sensor connecting the scissors to the board with some embroidery thread.
