MEDIATED EXTENSION OF SELF

WHAT AM I DREAMING OF

I modeled my dream space after the idea of the consistent journey towards the self. Our worlds are carefully crafted as we travel through the time and spaces of our environments resulting in multiple universes gravitating and orbiting through our own universal paths.

SPECIFICS

During this time I took heavy inspiration from Daft Punk’s Interstella 5555, featured in the digital screens within the floating ship. The story of loss of identity and the journey to find themselves after losing touch with who they are resonated during the creation of this universe.
When building out the universe, I found limitations on polygon count in the ready made assets provided by Mozilla Hub so I created and animated my own world in Cinema4D before exporting it out to Hub
