Plant Time

Time for Plant

This is the jade plant. It's also known as a friendship plant because it's very easy to split and gift to a friend of yours. It's extremely easy to take care of. It needs very little water and a decent amount of sun but can survive in medium light as well. They're a symbol of good luck and very enjoyable to take care of.
