Snack Ratings



Flamin' Hot Cheetos

Spicy n tasty. Nice and crunchy. I get really thirsty when I eat these because I can't eat spicy things but it still tastes really good. It's really good if you squeeze some fresh lime on top or dip it in a little queso

8/10



TJ's Mochi Rice Nuggets

These are my favorite snack actually. They're really crunchy and light and fluffy. I could eat a whole bag in a sitting if I had no discipline. They're surprisingly light for a mochi cracker and they're not too salty. I hear there's a spicy version too but those are apparently insanely spicy.

10000/10



The classic banana

You can't go wrong with this classic snack. Even the packaging is biodegradable. Especially good when it's a little bit green to balance the sweet and tart flavors. Recommend for breakfasts

7/10



Plain popcorn

Bruh. Why. I guess it's crunchy, but if you're looking for that, eat some celery or something.

1/10



Snyder's Pretzel Pieces, Honey Mustard & Onion

These are very very addicting. Eat at your own risk. They're tangy crunchy and covered in flavor. You like mustard? You like onion???? You will like this.

9/10
